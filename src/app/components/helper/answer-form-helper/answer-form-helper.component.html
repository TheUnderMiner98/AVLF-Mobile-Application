I need to take action after receiving an eviction notice

If you received an eviction notice, you'll need to file an answer within 7 days, excluding weekends and court holidays.

What would happen if I don't take action?

You will risk losing your case automatically if you file a late answer, do not file an answer, or file an improper legal defense.

Watch out for Improper Service

A Sheriff, Marshal, or Private Process Server, can legally serve an eviction notice, either in person or via tack and mail. If your landlord tries to serve you the warrant themselves, you did not receive proper service. Document the date, method, and who delivered your eviction notice.


If you believe the eviction notice wasn't properly served to you, please consult with a lawyer.
